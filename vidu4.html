NOUNS

A noun represents a person, place, animal, thing or an idea.

KINDS OF NOUN


Nouns are classified as follows :

Proper: It is the name of a particular object, whether person, place, animal, idea
or thing

Common: It is the name possessed by things in general.

Gollective: It is the name representing a groud of things.

Abstract: It is the name representing qualities, emotions, conditions or actions.

PRONOUNS

Apronoun is a word used in place of a noun. A pronoun is generally used to avoid
repetition of a noun.

KINDS OF PRONOUN

Pronouns are classified as follows:


Personal pronoun: It refers to a specific noun and used as a substitute of the noun
noun

Interrogative pronoun: It stands as the answer to the question about
person, place or a thing.

Indefinite pronouns:It is used to refer to person, thing or a place that are
unknown.

Relative pronouns: It relates to a noun, pronoun, word or expression
preceding it in the sentence, and at the same time connects two statements.

Reflexive pronouns: It usually folloows a noun or a pronoun and used to
emphasize the subject.
